### Recommended Site Structure (Sitemap)

**Main Navigation Menu** (top bar – keep it simple, 5–7 items max):
- Home
- About
- Services / How I Help (or Programs)
- Success Stories
- Blog / Resources
- Contact / Work With Me

**Optional additions** (as sub-pages or footer links):
- FAQ
- Free Resources / Lead Magnet (e.g., guide to time-restricted eating)

#### 1. Home Page (Landing Page)
Purpose: Grab attention in 5–10 seconds, show who you help and the transformation possible, build trust quickly.

Suggested sections (scrolling down):
- Hero Section: Bold headline like "Reset Your Health, Reclaim Your Energy – Feel Like Your Younger, Vibrant Self" + subheadline mentioning metabolic fitness, gut health, natural hormonal balance. Include your photo + primary CTA button ("Book Free Discovery Call").
- Quick Value Proposition: 3–4 icons/cards highlighting key benefits (e.g., "Improve Metabolic Fitness", "Master Time-Restricted Eating & Fasting", "Balance Hormones Naturally", "Optimize Gut Health & Detox").
- Why Choose Me / Passion snippet: Short version of your "Why I'm passionate about helping others" (personal hook – your story in 100–150 words).
- Featured Success Stories: 2–3 short client testimonials with before/after highlights or photos (if available).
- Services Overview: Teaser list of your main approaches (link to full Services page).
- CTA Section: "Ready to transform your health? Let's talk." + booking link.
- Footer trust builders: Certifications, "Food as Medicine" philosophy mention.

#### 2. About Page
Purpose: Build deep connection and credibility. Combine your provided "About me" and "Why I am passionate about helping others."

Suggested sections:
- Hero: Professional photo + tagline (e.g., "Helping You Reset to Your Healthiest, Most Energized Self").
- My Story: Personal background, health journey (if relevant), what led you to health coaching.
- Why I'm Passionate: Detailed explanation – your drive to help others reclaim vitality, inspired by personal/family experiences or seeing clients transform.
- My Philosophy / Approach: Bio-individuality (everyone is unique), Food as Medicine, holistic focus (metabolism, gut, hormones, detox, autophagy via fasting).
- Qualifications/Certifications: List any credentials.
- Photo gallery or timeline if it fits your story.
- CTA: "If my approach resonates, let's connect" → link to contact or booking.

#### 3. Services / How I Help / Programs Page
Purpose: Clearly explain your offerings and value. Group your numbered list into logical categories to avoid overwhelming visitors (13+ items can feel scattered).

Suggested structure:
- Hero: "Personalized Health Coaching to Reset Your Body & Life"
- Core Focus Areas: Use expandable accordions, cards, or tabs for scannability:
  1. Improve Metabolic Fitness
  2. Time-Restricted Eating & Fasting (including Autophagy benefits)
  3. Optimize Eating Patterns + Balance Your Plate
  4. Qualitize Your Food Choices (focus on quality over strict diets)
  5. Food as Medicine + Gut Health
  6. Detoxification Support
  7. Sleep Optimization
  8. Hormonal Balance Through Natural Eating
  9. Body Recomposition (fat loss + muscle gain sustainably)
  10. Bio-Individuality – Tailored to Your Unique Needs
  11. Reset Your Health to Your Younger Self (overarching promise)
- Programs/Packages: Describe 1:1 coaching tiers (e.g., 3-month reset program, 6-month deep transformation), group options if any, what’s included (sessions, support, resources).
- How It Works: Simple 3–5 step process (discovery call → assessment → personalized plan → ongoing support).
- CTA: Strong booking button.

#### 4. Success Stories / Client Results Page
Purpose: Social proof – one of the most convincing elements on health coach sites.

Suggested sections:
- Hero: "Real Transformations from Real People"
- Case Studies/Testimonials: 4–8 detailed stories (with permission), including client photos (before/after if appropriate), quotes, metrics (e.g., "Lost 25 lbs, reversed prediabetes, energy through the roof").
- Short testimonials carousel on other pages too.
- CTA: "Your success story could be next – book a call."

#### 5. Blog / Resources Page
Purpose: SEO, value-giving, establish expertise.

Suggested:
- Articles on your topics (e.g., "How Time-Restricted Eating Boosts Autophagy", "Gut Health 101", "Natural Ways to Balance Hormones").
- Free downloads: Lead magnets like "Plate Balancing Guide" or "Beginner’s Fasting Roadmap".
- Categories: Metabolic Health, Fasting, Gut & Detox, Sleep & Hormones, etc.

#### 6. Contact / Work With Me Page
Purpose: Make booking frictionless.

Suggested:
- Hero: "Ready to Reset Your Health?"
- Booking calendar embed (Calendly or similar) for free discovery calls.
- Contact form.
- FAQ: Common questions (e.g., "What is bio-individuality?", "How long until I see results?").
- Your email/phone if preferred.

### Additional Suggestions
- **Overall Design Tips**: Clean, modern, calming colors (greens, earth tones, whites). High-quality images of healthy food, nature, active people (avoid stocky feel). Mobile-responsive is essential.
- **Lead Capture**: Add a popup or banner for a free resource (e.g., "Download My Guide to Time-Restricted Eating").
- **Footer**: Include navigation links, social media, disclaimer (not medical advice), privacy policy.
- **Optional Add-Ons**: Podcast/Resources page if you create content, Shop for digital products (e.g., meal plans).

This structure is flexible – start minimal (Home, About, Services, Contact + Success Stories section) and expand. It positions you as a holistic, evidence-inspired coach focused on sustainable, natural resets.
